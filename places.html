Places to Visit in Nepal

Explore some of the most beautiful, adventurous, and culturally rich destinations in Nepal.

Pokhara





Pokhara is a serene lakeside city surrounded by the Annapurna mountain range. It's a gateway for trekkers and a relaxing escape with stunning natural beauty.

    Activities to Do in Pokhara:
  • Boating on Phewa Lake
  • Paragliding over the Himalayas
  • Hiking to World Peace Pagoda
  • Exploring underground caves like Gupteshwor

  • Exploring Durbar Squares and traditional Newari architecture
  • Trying authentic Nepali cuisine and local markets
  • Shop local handicrafts and artifacts
Mustang





Mustang is a remote, high-altitude desert region known for its unique Tibetan-influenced culture, mysterious caves, and spectacular landscapes.

    Activities to Do in Mustang:
  • Trekking the Upper Mustang Trail
  • Exploring ancient caves and monasteries
  • Visiting the walled city of Lo Manthang
  • Scenic jeep tours through rugged terrain
